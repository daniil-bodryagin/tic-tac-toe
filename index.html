Rules

Choose a side. Make moves to the cells. Make a line of 4 cells of your color in a row - vertically, horizontally or diagonally. Don't let the computer do the same.

And remember - Black move first!

Controls

The cell to move is chosen by voice. The name of the cell consists of two words - the name of the column and the name of the line.

  • A - alpha (алфа)
  • B - bravo (бравоу)
  • C - charlie (чали)
  • D - delta (делта)
  • E - echo (экоу)

For example, the upper right corner is "eco alpha".

Press the "Listen", record your voice command. You can re record command if something went wrong. When you get satisfied, press "Confirm" to make your move.

Or if you don't want to use voice control - just press the button below to switch onto mouse or touchscreen.
